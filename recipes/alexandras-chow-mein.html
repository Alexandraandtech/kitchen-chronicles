Alexandra's Chow Mein

Ingredients

  • 2 Teaspoons Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Rice Vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoon Sesame Oil
  • 2 Boneless Chicken Thighs or Breast (cut into cubes)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 Tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Oyster Sauce
  • 2 Stalks Celery Chopped
  • 1 Yellow Onion (diced)
  • 1 Cup Mushrooms
  • 1 Cup Snap Peas
  • 4 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 1/4 Cup of Corn starch
  • Maruchan Ramen Noodles (3 or 4 bags)
  • Oil

Description

This is my take on a delicious Chicken Chow Mein made with Maruchan style noodles.

Steps

Ramen Noodles:
  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add ramen noodles to water for approx. 2 mins and drain boodles
  3. In a pan add oil, once oil is hot add noodles

Chow Mein:
  1. Marinate Chicken: In a bowl combine soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil. Add chicken and toss and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, add oil and garlic. Once pan is hot add vegetables. Toss for two minutes. Add soy sauce or seasoning to taste. Mix in chicken and cook till chicken is 50%-75% done.
  3. In deep pan, add chicken broth and turn to medium. Add chicken and veggies to broth, let simmer 10-15 minutes.
  4. Combine 1/4 cup corn starch, slowly add to water till it makes a smooth paste, add to pot and stir 2 minutes.
  5. One thickened, pour over noodles, add oyster sauce
